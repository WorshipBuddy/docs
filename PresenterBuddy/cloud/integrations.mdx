PresenterBuddy has integrations with other software to make your experience more seamless.

# API.Bible
Quickly import a Bible from over 200 available Bible versions. Note: You have to be connected to the internet to be able to use them, or you can import a local scripture file.

- API.Bible

# Planning Center
PresenterBuddy allows you to connect your Planning Center account, and automatically sync your services planned ahead of time. Go to the "Connections" tab in the settings, and connect your account, then PresenterBuddy will automatically download any future services within one week on startup.

- Planning Center

# Pixabay & Unsplash
Search in a large and free library of images and videos online without leaving the application.

- Pixabay
- Unsplash
