Once you've installed PresenterBuddy and set up your first presentation, here's how to get the most out of it.

## Managing Slides

- **Add a slide**: Click the **+** button to add a new slide to your presentation.
- **Reorder slides**: Drag and drop slides in the sidebar to rearrange them.
- **Edit a slide**: Double-click a slide to edit its content.
- **Delete a slide**: Select a slide and press **Delete** or use the right-click menu.

## Displaying Content

- **Song lyrics**: Add lyrics slide by slide, with each slide representing a verse or chorus.
- **Scriptures**: Add Bible verses as text slides.
- **Images & video**: Insert media files as background or standalone slides.

## Live Presentation

- Use the **arrow keys** or click slides in the sidebar to navigate.
- Press **Escape** to black out the output screen.
- Use the **Output** toggle to quickly enable or disable the live display.

## Importing Content

PresenterBuddy supports importing content from common formats so you can bring in songs and presentations you already have.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Action | Shortcut |
|---|---|
| Next slide | → or Space |
| Previous slide | ← |
| Black out screen | Escape |
| Toggle output | Ctrl/Cmd + O |
