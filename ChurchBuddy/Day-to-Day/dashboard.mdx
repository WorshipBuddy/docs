# Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your personal homepage when you log into ChurchBuddy on the web. It provides an overview of your schedule, upcoming assignments, and organization information.

<Info>
**Announcements** are visible on the mobile **Home** tab, not on the web Dashboard page. Manage announcements under **Organization → Announcements**.
</Info>

<Info>
**Platform**: Web. The mobile app equivalent is the **Home** tab. See [Mobile App](/ChurchBuddy/Day-to-Day/app).
</Info>

## What You'll See

1. **Welcome Header** — `Welcome back, {first name}!` with your organization name in the subtitle
2. **Availability Button** - Quick access to set unavailability
3. **My Upcoming Schedule Widget** - Services where you're scheduled
4. **Upcoming Events Widget** - Upcoming events in your organization
5. **Volunteer Opportunities Widget** - Positions you can volunteer for (if teams allow volunteering)
6. **Quick Actions** (Owner/Admin only) - Add service, add event, manage teams, view people

## Pending Assignment Confirmations

If teams you serve on require accept/decline confirmation, **Accept** and **Decline** buttons appear inline on each service card in **My Upcoming Schedule** on web.

<Info>
Mobile uses a dedicated **Action Required** section on the **Home** tab for the same flow.
</Info>

## Setting Your Availability

1. Click **Schedule Your Availability** on the Dashboard
2. A calendar popup appears
3. Click dates to mark as unavailable (red circle)
4. Click again to remove the mark
5. Changes auto-save

<Info>
Unavailability helps schedulers see warnings when assigning you. See [Managing Your Availability](/ChurchBuddy/Day-to-Day/availability).
</Info>

## Viewing Your Upcoming Services

Each service card shows:

- **Service Name**, **Date and Time**, **Location**
- **Your Assigned Position(s)** and **Team(s)**
- **Confirmation status** (Accepted, Declined, Pending) if accept/decline is enabled

Click a service card to go to the Calendar section.

## Volunteer Opportunities

Shows open positions on teams with **Allow people to volunteer** enabled:

- **Available Positions** - Open slots you can claim
- **Already Volunteered** - Positions you've claimed
- **Volunteer** button to sign up

## Quick Actions (Owner/Admin Only)

- **Add Service** - Create a new service
- **Add Event** - Create a new event
- **Manage Teams** - Go to Teams management
- **View People** - Go to the People section

## Navigation

From the Dashboard, use the top navigation:

| Nav Item | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Dashboard** | This page |
| **People** | People Dashboard, People, Groups, Workflows, Forms, Check-in (Check-in: Owner/Admin only) |
| **Calendar** | Calendar, Services, Events, Rooms, Resources (see role matrix below) |
| **Organization** | Settings, Announcements, Integrations, SS, Teams (**Team Admins: Teams sub-tab only**) |
| **Streaming** | Live streams (when any integration is connected — YouTube or Zoom) |
| **WorshipBuddy** | Music albums (when you have access) |

<Info>
**People** top nav is visible to **Owners, Organization Admins, and Team Admins**. Schedulers and regular Members do not see the People nav.

**Teams** are managed via **Organization → Teams** sub-tab (redirects to the Teams page), not as a separate top-nav item.
</Info>

### Calendar Sub-Tab Access by Role

| Role | Visible Calendar Sub-Tabs |
|------|---------------------------|
| Owner / Org Admin | Calendar, Services, Events, Rooms, Resources |
| Team Admin / Scheduler | Calendar, Services |
| Member / Viewer | Calendar only (Rooms, Resources, Events, and Services sub-tabs hidden) |
