# Organization Details

The Organization section contains multiple sub-tabs for managing your organization. The **Organization Details** sub-tab lets Owners and Organization Admins manage organization information, view the Organization ID, and transfer ownership.

## Accessing Organization Details

1. Click **Organization** in the top navigation
2. Click the **Organization Details** sub-tab
3. You must be an Owner or Organization Admin

<Info>
The **Organization** section contains these sub-tabs:
- **Announcements** - Manage organization announcements
- **Organization Details** - Organization information and ownership (this page)
- **Integrations** - YouTube, Text in Church, PresenterBuddy, and other connections
- **Sunday School** - Configure Sunday School module
- **Teams** - Opens the Teams management page
- **Giving** - Visible only when Stripe is enabled on the server **and** Stripe onboarding is complete (`enabled: true` in giving config)
</Info>

## Organization Information

### Viewing Organization ID

Your Organization ID is displayed at the top of the Organization Details sub-tab. Share this ID so people can join your organization.

**To copy the Organization ID:**
1. Click **Copy** next to the Organization ID
2. Share it with people you want to invite (they use it in the mobile app or web join flow)

### Editing Organization Details

You can update:

1. **Organization Name** (required)
2. **Organization Type** (required)
3. **Address** fields — Street, line 2, city, state/province, zip/postal code
4. **Parent Organization ID** (optional) — Link this org to a parent org

Click **Save Changes** when done.

## Transferring Organization Ownership

**Owners only** can transfer ownership to another member.

<Warning>
Transferring ownership cannot be undone. The new owner will have full control, and you will become a regular member.
</Warning>

1. Go to **People** and open the person who should become owner
2. Click **Edit Positions & Teams**
3. Click **Transfer Ownership** and confirm

2. **Protect your Organization ID** - Only share with people you want to invite
