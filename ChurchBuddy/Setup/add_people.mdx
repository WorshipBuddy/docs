# Adding People to Your Organization

Before you can schedule services, you need to add people to your organization. ChurchBuddy offers two ways to add people: bulk import via Excel file or manual entry one at a time.

## Adding People Overview

When you add people to your organization:

- They receive an invitation email to join ChurchBuddy
- They can create an account (if they don't have one) or log in with their existing account
- They'll be able to see services they're scheduled for
- You can assign them to teams and positions
- You can set their permissions and roles

<Info>
**Note**: People can view any service they're scheduled for, regardless of their permissions. This ensures they can always see their assignments.
</Info>

## Method 1: Import via Excel File

Bulk importing is the fastest way to add multiple people at once.

### Step 1: Download the Template

1. <a href="https://pb.worshipbuddy.org/Import%20People.xlsx" download>Download the Excel import template</a>
2. Open the template in Excel or Google Sheets

### Step 2: Fill in the Template

The template has three columns:
- **First Name** (required)
- **Last Name** (required)
- **Email** (required)

**Important guidelines:**
- **Start at row 2** - Row 1 contains headers, don't modify it
- **All three fields are required** for each person
- Enter one person per row
- Make sure email addresses are correct (they'll receive invitations)
- Don't leave any required fields blank

### Step 3: Import the File

1. Go to **People** in your organization
2. Click **"Import People"** or **"Add People"**
3. Choose **"Import from Excel"** or drag and drop your file
4. Select your completed Excel file
5. Click **"Import People"**

<Warning>
**Important**: The import process may take a moment. Don't click "Import People" multiple times, even if it seems like nothing is happening. Wait for the import to complete.
</Warning>

### Step 4: Verify Import

After importing, you should see:
- A confirmation message showing how many people were added
- The new people appearing in your People list
- Any errors or issues with specific rows (if applicable)

## Method 2: Manual Entry

If you only need to add a few people, or prefer to enter them one at a time:

1. Go to **People** in your organization
2. Click **"Add Person"** or **"Import People"** → **"Enter Manually"**
3. Fill in the required information:
   - **First Name** (required)
   - **Last Name** (required)
   - **Email** (required)
4. Click **"Add Row"** to add another person
5. Repeat until all people are entered
6. Click **"Import People"** or **"Add People"**

<Warning>
**Important**: The import process may take a moment. Don't click the button multiple times, even if it seems like nothing is happening.
</Warning>

## What Happens After Adding People

Once people are added, several things happen automatically:

1. **They're Added to Your Organization** - People are immediately added to your organization's member list with a "pending" status
   - They appear in your People list right away
   - They have a "pending" position until they log in for the first time
   - You can assign them to positions even while they're pending

2. **Account Creation** - If they don't have a ChurchBuddy account, one is created automatically
   - Their account is linked to your organization
   - They can log in immediately using their email address

3. **Invitation Email Sent** - Each person receives an email invitation that says:

   > Hi [First Name],
   >
   > You've been invited to join [Your Org Name] on ChurchBuddy!
   >
   > ChurchBuddy is a comprehensive church management platform that helps organize and manage every aspect of ministry. It's more than just scheduling—it's a complete platform for church management.
   >
   > With ChurchBuddy, you can:
   > - View your schedule and assignments
   > - Set your availability to help schedulers plan
   > - See announcements and important updates
   > - Check in to services and events
   > - Manage your profile and preferences
   > - And much more!
   >
   > To get started, click the link below and enter the Organization ID: [Your Org ID]
   > https://church.worshipbuddy.org/
   >
   > You can also download the ChurchBuddy mobile app for iOS and Android from our website:
   > https://worshipbuddy.org/schedule-buddy
   >
   > If you don't already have an account, you'll be prompted to create one when you log in.
   >
   > - The ChurchBuddy Team, on behalf of [Your Org Name]

4. **Activation When They Log In** - When they log in and access your organization:
   - The "pending" status is automatically removed
   - They become an active member
   - They can see their assignments and set their availability

<Info>
**Note**: People are already members of your organization immediately after being added. The "pending" status is just a marker that they haven't logged in yet. You can assign them to positions and schedule them even while they're pending.
</Info>

## Assigning Positions

After adding people, you'll want to assign them to positions so they can be scheduled:

1. Go to **People**
2. Click on the person you want to assign
3. In the **"Positions"** section, you'll see all available positions from all teams
4. Check the boxes next to positions they can serve in
   - Example: Check "Lead Singer" and "Piano" if they can do both
5. Click **"Update Person"** to save

<Info>
**Tip**: You can assign multiple positions to one person. This allows them to be scheduled for different roles depending on the service's needs.
</Info>

## Best Practices

1. **Verify Email Addresses** - Double-check emails before importing to ensure invitations are delivered
2. **Import in Batches** - For large organizations, consider importing in smaller groups (50-100 at a time)
3. **Keep Template Updated** - Save your import template with your organization's standard format
4. **Assign Positions Promptly** - Assign positions soon after adding people so they can be scheduled
5. **Communicate Expectations** - Let people know they'll receive an invitation email and should accept it

## Troubleshooting

**People not receiving invitation emails:**
- Check that email addresses are correct
- Ask them to check spam/junk folders
- Verify the email wasn't blocked by their email provider
- Note: They're already added to your organization even if they don't receive the email - they just need to log in

**People showing as "pending":**
- This is normal - they're already members, just haven't logged in yet
- The "pending" status is automatically removed when they log in
- You can still assign them to positions and schedule them while pending

**Import errors:**
- Make sure all required fields (first name, last name, email) are filled in
- Check for duplicate email addresses (each person needs a unique email)
- Verify the Excel file format matches the template
- Try importing a smaller batch to identify problematic rows

**Can't assign positions:**
- Make sure you've created teams with positions first
- Verify the person has been successfully added to your organization
- Check that you have permission to edit people (Owner or Admin)

**Need to add more people later:**
- You can import additional people anytime using the same process
- New people will receive invitation emails just like the first batch
- Existing people won't receive duplicate invitations
