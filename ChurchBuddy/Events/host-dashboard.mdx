# Host Dashboard

The Host Dashboard gives housing hosts a dedicated view of their assigned guests, including guest details, check-in status, and stay dates.

## Overview

The Host Dashboard shows hosts:

- **Assigned Guests** - All guests staying at their location
- **Guest Details** - Names, contact info, and special needs
- **Check-in Status** - Whether guests have checked in
- **Stay Dates** - Arrival and departure dates
- **Capacity** - How many guests vs. available space

## Accessing the Host Dashboard

Hosts access their dashboard via a direct link:

```
/host-dashboard/?org=YOUR_ORG_ID&event=EVENT_ID
```

Or through the event's housing section when logged in.

## Dashboard Features

### Summary Statistics

At the top, hosts see:

- **Total Guests** - Number of guests assigned
- **Checked In** - How many have arrived
- **Expected** - How many are still expected

### Guest Cards

Each guest displays:

- **Name** - Guest's full name
- **Check-in Status** - Badge showing checked in or not
- **Stay Dates** - When they arrive and depart
- **Contact Info** - Phone number or email
- **Dietary Restrictions** - Any food allergies or requirements
- **Special Needs** - Accessibility or other requirements
- **Notes** - Any additional information

## Using the Dashboard

### Before Guests Arrive

1. Review your assigned guest list
2. Note any dietary restrictions or special needs
3. Check arrival dates and times
4. Prepare accommodations accordingly

### When Guests Check In

The dashboard updates to show check-in status:

- **Green badge** - Guest has checked in to the event
- **Yellow badge** - Guest hasn't checked in yet

<Info>
**Note**: Check-in status reflects event check-in, not arrival at your home. Guests check in through the event's main check-in process.
</Info>

### Guest Information Details

Click on a guest card to see full details:

- Complete contact information
- All dietary restrictions and allergies
- Special accommodation needs
- Notes from event organizers
- Emergency contact (if provided)

## Print View

Print your guest list for reference:

1. The dashboard is print-optimized
2. Use browser print function
3. Get a clean list of all guest information

## Communicating with Guests

- Tap phone numbers to call directly
- Tap email addresses to compose messages
- Share your contact info through the event

## Best Practices

1. **Review early** - Check your guest list before they arrive
2. **Accommodate needs** - Pay attention to dietary and special needs
3. **Be available** - Guests may need to contact you
4. **Coordinate arrivals** - Know when guests are expected
5. **Prepare spaces** - Have accommodations ready based on guest count
