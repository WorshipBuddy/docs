# Transportation Management

Coordinate ground transportation: vehicles, drivers, routes, and passenger assignments, with live flight tracking, a live GPS vehicle map, driver ETAs, and QR check-in using registration QR codes.

## Setting Up Transportation

1. Open the **Event Dashboard**
2. On **Overview**, use the **Add a module** card to enable **Transportation**
3. Open the **Transportation** module from the rail
4. Add vehicles and drivers, then create routes

The Overview shows a **Get transportation set up** checklist (add vehicles, add drivers, create routes, assign passengers) that disappears once everything is in place.

## Vehicles

Click **+ Add Vehicle** and set:

- **Vehicle Type** and **Vehicle Name/Identifier**
- **Total seats (including driver)**
- **License Plate**, **VIN**, **Notes**
- Optional **Luggage capacity** (checked bags, carry-on bags)
- Optional **GPS tracker & live map** (tracker IMEI, protocol, live-tracking toggle, map color)

You cannot delete a vehicle assigned to active routes. Seat capacity includes the driver.

## Drivers

Click **+ Add Driver** and set name, email, phone, an optional linked personal vehicle, and notes.

### Driver Dashboard

Send drivers their dashboard link:

```
/driver-dashboard/?org={org_id}&event={event_id}&driver={driver_id}
```

It shows their routes, vehicle, pickup/drop-off, passengers, and a Google Maps route. When passengers have flights, each shows a live flight status pill and timing, and the dashboard can show a **live ETA to the drop-off** from the driver's current location. See [Driver Dashboard](/ChurchBuddy/Events/driver-dashboard).

## Routes

Click **+ Create Route** and configure:

- Trip type: **Arrival** or **Departure**
- **Vehicle** and **Driver** (both required)
- **Starting point** (where the driver leaves from)
- Ordered **Pickup Locations** (Airport, Bus Station, Train Station, or Address) — use **+ Add stop**
- **Drop-off Location**
- **Departure Time** and **Arrival Time** (a smart-scheduling helper can estimate times from your stops)
- **Route Name** and optional **Capacity** override
- **Send QR tickets & reminder emails** — passengers get a QR ticket and reminders

Passengers appear inline on each route card under **Assigned Passengers (N)**.

## Assigning Passengers

You have several ways to assign:

- **Drag-and-drop** — Drag registrants from **Unassigned Registrants** onto a route
- **Assign** — Click **Assign** on an unassigned card to pick a route from a menu
- **Assign all shown** — Bulk-assign everyone currently shown, filling routes and stopping at seat capacity
- **Search** — Filter unassigned registrants by name or email

Bulk assignment enforces seat capacity and marks routes **Full** when they fill. Passengers use their **registration QR code** for transportation check-in — there are no separate route QR codes.

## Flight Tracking

The **Flight Data** tab reads arrival, departure, and luggage details from registration responses and adds **live flight status** (via public ADS-B data) at no cost:

- A colored **live status pill** per flight (Scheduled, At gate, Departed, In flight, Landing soon, Landed) with an **ETA**
- **Delay detection** — when a flight is running 15+ minutes late, the row flags the delay and offers a **Notify driver** button that emails the route's driver
- **Manage Airports** — maintain the event's airport list (code, label, coordinates) used for ETAs
- **Live Flight Map** — opens the full flight dashboard for the event

### Airport question on registration

When transportation is enabled, the public registration form can include an **Arrival Airport** dropdown built from your event's airport list, with an "Airport not listed" option that reveals a free-text field. This keeps flight data clean for tracking.

## Live Vehicle Map

If your vehicles have GPS trackers, the **Live Vehicle Map** shows them in real time:

- Live positions from an OBD2/GPS tracker (connected through Traccar using each tracker's IMEI)
- **Zone alerts** — email people when a tracked vehicle enters, leaves, or exits geofenced zones
- Map controls for **Recenter**, **Refresh**, and **Full screen**

The GPS tracker fields live in the vehicle modal's optional **GPS tracker & live map** section.

## Transportation Scanner

Share the scanner link for QR check-in at pickup/drop-off:

```
/scan/?mode=transportation&org={org_id}&event={event_id}
```

<Info>
The legacy `/transportation-scanner/` link still works and redirects to `/scan/?mode=transportation`.
</Info>

1. Enter your name
2. Select the route
3. Scan registration QR codes
4. The system verifies the assignment and records check-in

## Route Notifications

On a route with a driver and passengers, **Send tickets and route info** emails QR tickets to passengers and the route details to the driver. Flight delays can be sent to a driver individually with **Notify driver** from the Flight Data tab.

## Best Practices

1. Create vehicles and drivers before routes
2. Add the **Arrival Airport** question so flight tracking has clean data
3. Add tracker IMEIs to vehicles to light up the live map and driver ETAs
4. Use **Assign all shown** to fill routes quickly, then adjust
5. Watch **Flight Data** for delays and use **Notify driver** proactively

## Troubleshooting

**No live flight status:** Confirm registrants entered flight numbers and the arrival airport, and that the airport is in **Manage Airports**.

**Live map empty:** Confirm the vehicle has a tracker IMEI, live tracking is enabled, and the tracker is reporting through Traccar.

**Can't delete vehicle:** Remove it from all routes first.

**QR not scanning:** The passenger must use their registration QR and be assigned to the selected route.

**Driver dashboard empty:** Verify the `driver=` ID and route assignments.
