With WorshipBuddy, you can easily transpose any song to any key, create and organize worship sets, access a vast collection of worship songs and lyrics, and even add and manage your own songs and arrangements.

The bottom navigation bar includes four sections:
- Songbook: View lyrics for all available songs.
- Music Library: Listen to available recordings.
- Sets: Create and share worship sets with your team.
- Settings: Customize the app to fit your preferences.
