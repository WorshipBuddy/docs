In the Settings page, you can customize your WorshipBuddy experience:
- Edit your profile or sign out of your account
- Switch app theme between Auto, Light, or Dark
- Adjust swipe sensitivity for the songbook (helpful if swiping feels too sensitive or unresponsive)
- Change the font and font size for lyrics and chord display
- Set your default startup screen (Songbook or Music Library)
- Enable “Keep Awake” to prevent your phone from auto-locking during use
- Auto-download liked songs (and delete them with the trash icon)
- Hide or show chords in the song view
- Display chords as numbers (Arabic or Roman numerals)
- View the change log for app updates
- Visit our website, contact support, request a feature, or get involved using the links at the bottom
