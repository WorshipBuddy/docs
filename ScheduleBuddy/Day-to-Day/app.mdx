Users can download the ScheduleBuddy app from the [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/ca/app/schedulebuddy/id6747605543?uo=2) and [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.worshipbuddycompany.ScheduleBuddyApp&pcampaignid=web_share).

The app gives users the ability to set their availability, see what services they are scheduled for and view all upcoming services for the organization.
