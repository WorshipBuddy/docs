# Organization Settings

The Settings page allows Owners and Organization Admins to manage organization-wide settings, information, and preferences.

## Accessing Settings

1. Go to your organization's main page
2. Click **"Settings"** in the navigation bar
3. You must be an Owner or Organization Admin to access Settings

## Organization Information

### Viewing Organization ID

Your Organization ID is displayed at the top of the Settings page. This ID is used to invite people to join your organization.

**To copy the Organization ID:**
1. Click the **"Copy"** button next to the Organization ID
2. Share this ID with people you want to invite

<Info>
**Tip**: Share the Organization ID with new members so they can join your organization through the mobile app or web interface.
</Info>

### Editing Organization Details

You can update the following information:

1. **Organization Name** (required)
2. **Organization Type** (required) - Choose from:
   - Church
   - Ministry
   - Organization
   - Other
3. **Address** - Street address
4. **Address Line 2** - Additional address information
5. **City**
6. **State/Province**
7. **Zip/Postal Code**

**To update organization information:**
1. Make your changes in the "Organization Information" section
2. Click **"Save Changes"**
3. A confirmation message will appear when saved

## Transferring Organization Ownership

Owners can transfer ownership of the organization to another member.

<Warning>
**Warning**: Transferring ownership cannot be undone. The new owner will have full control of the organization, and you will become a regular member.
</Warning>

**To transfer ownership:**
1. Scroll to the "Transfer Ownership" section
2. Select the new owner from the dropdown (only shows current organization members)
3. Click **"Transfer Ownership"**
4. Confirm the transfer

<Info>
**Note**: Only the current Owner can transfer ownership. Organization Admins cannot transfer ownership.
</Info>

## Other Settings

Additional settings may be available depending on your organization's configuration and integrations. These may include:

- WorshipBuddy integration settings
- BibleBuddy integration settings
- Notification preferences
- Export settings

See the relevant integration documentation for details on those specific settings.

## Saving Changes

- Changes are saved immediately when you click **"Save Changes"**
- A success message will confirm your changes were saved
- If there's an error, an error message will appear with details

## Best Practices

1. **Keep information current** - Update organization details if they change
2. **Protect your Organization ID** - Only share it with people you want to invite
3. **Choose ownership carefully** - Only transfer ownership to trusted members
4. **Review settings regularly** - Check settings periodically to ensure they're still accurate
