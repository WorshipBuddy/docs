# Understanding Organizations
An organization (or org) is entity with multiple teams (or ministries) within it.

# Creating an Org
To create an org, follow the below instructions:

1. Log into ScheduleBuddy
2. Select "Start Org"
3. Fill out the information for your organization.
    1. Organization Name: What is the name of your organization?
    2. Organization Type: What type of organization are you?
    3. Address: What is the address of your organization?

You have now created a new organization! You should now see your org on your org page along with a unique ID number. This ID number is what you'll share to allow other users to join your org.
