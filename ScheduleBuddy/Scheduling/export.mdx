# Exporting Schedules

You can export your schedule to multiple formats for sharing, printing, or backup purposes. ScheduleBuddy supports exporting to Word documents and Excel files.

## Accessing Export

1. Go to the **Services** page
2. Click the **"Export Schedule"** button
3. A popup will appear with export options

## Selecting Services to Export

In the export popup:

1. A calendar preview shows all your services
2. Check the checkbox next to each service you want to include in the export
3. Use **"Select All"** to include all services
4. Use **"Clear"** to deselect all services

<Info>
**Tip**: You can export just upcoming services, past services, or a custom selection based on your needs.
</Info>

## Export Formats

### Export to Word

Creates a Word document (.docx) with your schedule.

**Format:**
- Each team appears on its own page
- Services are organized chronologically
- Shows date, time, location, and all assignments

**Steps:**
1. Select the services you want to export
2. Click **"Export to Word"**
3. The file will download automatically
4. Open it in Microsoft Word or any compatible word processor

<Info>
**Tip**: Word export is great for printing schedules or sharing with people who prefer Word documents.
</Info>

### Export to Excel

Creates an Excel spreadsheet (.xlsx) with your schedule.

**Format:**
- Each team appears as a separate sheet/tab
- Services are listed in rows
- Columns show date, time, location, and assignments

**Steps:**
1. Select the services you want to export
2. Click **"Export to Excel"**
3. The file will download automatically
4. Open it in Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, or any compatible spreadsheet program

<Info>
**Tip**: Excel export is great for data analysis, filtering, or importing into other systems.
</Info>

## Export Permissions

Only users with scheduling permissions can export:
- Owners
- Organization Admins
- Team Admins (for their teams)
- Schedulers (for their teams)

## Export Best Practices

1. **Export regularly** - Create backups of your schedules periodically
2. **Include date ranges** - Export specific time periods (month, quarter, etc.) for easier organization
3. **Use appropriate format** - Choose Word for printing, Excel for data work (you can save Word files as PDF if needed)
4. **Name files clearly** - When downloading, save with descriptive names like "Schedule_January_2025.docx"
5. **Review before sharing** - Check the exported file to ensure it looks correct before distributing

## Sharing Exported Schedules

After exporting, you can:
- **Email** the file to team members
- **Print** the schedule for physical distribution
- **Upload** to cloud storage for team access
- **Share** via messaging apps or collaboration tools

<Warning>
**Privacy Note**: Be careful when sharing exported schedules. They contain personal information (names, emails) that should be handled according to your organization's privacy policy.
</Warning>
