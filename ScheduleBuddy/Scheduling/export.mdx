You can export your schedule to a Word doc or an Excel. When exporting to Word, each team will export to it's own page. When exporting to Excel, it exports each team as a seprate sheet.

To export your schedule, follow the below instructions:

1. Press "Export Schedule"
2. Select which services you'd like to export by checking the check box for the service
3. To export to Word, press "Export to Word". To export to Excel, press "Export to Excel"
