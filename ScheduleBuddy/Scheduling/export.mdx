You can export your schedule to a Word document or an Excel file. When exporting to Word, each team will export to its own page. When exporting to Excel, it exports each team as a separate sheet.

To export your schedule, follow the instructions below:

1. Press "Export Schedule"
2. Select which services you'd like to export by checking the checkbox for the service
3. To export to Word, press "Export to Word". To export to Excel, press "Export to Excel"
